Coding

Coding has been my passion since I was 8 years old, a love that began with the thrill of creating something out of nothing but a basic laptop with Visual Basic and NET. As I grew, so did my hunger to learn more—moving from web design to roblox game developing and Lua, and then to NodeJS, each new language a gateway to endless possibilities. Python, which I started exploring in 2023, has become my latest obsession, especially as I dive into neural networks with Keras and TensorFlow. Machine learning feels like the future, and I’m determined to be a part of it, building a portfolio that not only showcases my skills but reflects my journey. For me, coding is more than just writing lines of code—it’s the thrill of solving problems and helping people, the excitement of learning new things, and the pure joy of creation.
